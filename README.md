## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

#### 1. Why does the app ask for Camera and Photo permissions?
The camera is used to process depth information in real-time. Photo access is required only when you choose to save a captured depth map to your device.

#### 2. Does this app support my device?
The AI mode works on most modern iPhones. The LiDAR mode requires a device equipped with a LiDAR scanner (iPhone 12 Pro or later, iPad Pro 2020 or later).

#### 3. How do I improve depth accuracy?
Ensure there is sufficient lighting for AI mode, or stay within 5 meters of the subject when using LiDAR mode.
